Baklava

Description

This baklava recipe is simple and easy. Serve it in cupcake papers. It freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chopped nuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 (16 ounce) package phyllo dough
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Â½ cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish..
  2. Toss together nuts and cinnamon. Unroll phyllo and cut the whole stack in half to fit the dish.
    Cover phyllo with a damp cloth while assembling the baklava, to keep it from drying out.
  3. While baklava is baking, combine sugar and water in a small saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil.
    Stir in honey, vanilla, and lemon zest; reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes..
